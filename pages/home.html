Welcome to Hamilton Tamil Sangam!

Established in May 2021, our journey began with a small gathering of Tamil-speaking families enjoying outdoor potlucks. As our community flourished, we embraced the growing number of families by organizing get-togethers in cozy small halls. Eventually, our events transitioned to larger community halls to accommodate our vibrant and expanding community. Join us as we celebrate our culture, foster connections, and create lasting memories together.

About Us

Hamilton Tamil Sangam (HTS) began as an intimate outdoor gathering of Tamil-speaking families. As our community grew, we expanded from small halls to larger community venues to accommodate our flourishing membership.

HTS serves as a vital connection point for the Tamil community in the Hamilton area, Ontario. We create opportunities for families to meet, children to interact, and members to share experiences, fostering a strong sense of cultural unity and understanding.

In 2023, HTS achieved an important milestone by registering as a Non-Profit organization. Our mission is to actively promote, preserve, and celebrate the rich cultural and linguistic heritage of Tamil Nadu and its people.

Operating independently of any religious or political affiliations, HTS is dedicated solely to enriching the social and cultural life of Hamilton's Tamil community, guided by our motto:

"தமிழால் இணைவோம்! அறிவால் உயர்வோம்!"
